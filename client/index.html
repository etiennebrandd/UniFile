Calendar

With so many things going on at university, it is difficult to keep on top of things. With UniFiles calendar, you are able to keep track of all those classes, exams and events in one place, so you'll never miss a deadline

UniFile's calendar also supports integration with Google Calendar, with Outlook Calendar integration in the works. This means that you can have all of your calendars and timetables in one place - no more jumping between apps to see your classes!

Budgeting

Most apps nowadays simply track your budget. You manually put in what you spend, and it tells you how much you have left for the week.

UniFile takes a different approach by doing the planning for you. We give you a range of categories and you rank them from most important to least. Have an expensive degree which requires specialised equipment? We'll deduct money from the least important priority to cover the expense

With integration across the rest of UniFile, and banking integration in the works, you can sleep easy knowing UniFIle has you covered

Meal Planning

For a lot of people, university is the first time they will be cooking for themselves. We take a lot of the guesswork out of it by planning the weeks meals for you, in co-operation with our budget app.

There is also the option to input a few of the odd ingredients you have spare in the cupboard to make an entirely new dish!
